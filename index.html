Film Design

This exhibition is a diverse display of design in film. These works present an investigation of graphics, sets, objects and parts of design that make a film what it is. The exhbition pamphlets have notes and essays from some designers, a rumination on the art of film design. This show continues in MOA PS3.










Featured Film Design: Inertia, Dorsal Raven, The Blacklist, Guild, Steadied, The Seventh Moon, Darkest Vision, Second
